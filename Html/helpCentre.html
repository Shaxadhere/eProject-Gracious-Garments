Popular Questions


Are all products on Gracious Garments original and genuine?


Yes, we are committed for offering our customers only 100% genuine and original products. We also take all necessary actions to ensure this. Any seller found to be selling non-genuine products is immediately delisted from Gracious Garments. Please send us an email on admin@graciousgarments.pk if you think a product listed on our website does not fulfill these standards.


How do I place an order?


Shopping on Gracious Garments is easy! Once you have found the product you want to buy, just follow the steps below:

  • Click on ’buy now’, to add this product to your cart
  • Click on ’Go to next step’ in the top right corner
  • You will then need to fill in your contact details and preferred shipping address

How can I get a refund or replacement for my product?


  • Step 1: Contact us

    Call us to create your return request, select the product you wish to return on the return form that arrived with your order.

  • Step 2: Pack your product

    Pack your product according to the return conditions Include all tags, accessories or free gifts you received

  • Step 3: Return your product

    If you requested a pickup, we will contact you to arrange a pickup time If you selected a drop-off: Please drop your package at any TCS station and attach the address label included with your package on the box (no shipping charges will apply)

Are there any hidden costs or charges if I order from Gracious Garment?


There are no hidden costs or charges when you order from Gracious Garments. All costs are 100% visible at the end of the checkout process.
