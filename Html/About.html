About Us

Gracious Garments provides a one-stop, enhanced shopping experience to consumers online, for their daily grocery needs and planned purchases of housewares, home textiles, toys, gifts, etc. Currently it operates in Karachi offering 24/7 deliveries and customer support with short-term plans to expand its operations nationwide.

Gracious Garments operates it’s own dedicated and stocked fulfilment centre along with a fleet of vehicles to provide express deliveries, daily time slotted deliveries and overnight order fulfilment.

Currently operating in beta phase, Gracious Garments has already forged valuable business relationships with majority of brands in CPG category and is moving rapidly to expanding the number of SKU’s available for its customers.

    Business Objectives
  • Seamless Shopping Experience
  • Largest Product Selection
  • Dedicated Customer Support

A team of talented and dedicated experts in the the fields of e-commerce, digital media, supply chain, logistics, customer services, finance and operations is already onboard to make this venture a resounding success and a case study for other organisations to replicate in order to become successful in Pakistan and beyond.

Gracious Garments also provides businesses with an opportunity for organisations to setup corporate accounts to manage purchases of business consumables and non-durable items. Since Gracious Garments is a registered and compliant business entity, all purchases made through Gracious Garments can be treated as documented purchases under the prevailing tax provisions.
