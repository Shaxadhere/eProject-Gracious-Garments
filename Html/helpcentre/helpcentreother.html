How can I sign-up as a customer on Gracious Garments?


  • Start by clicking on “My Account” and then “sign up” in the top left corner of your screen.
  • You will then be asked to provide some basic information.
  • When you have provided the required information, click submit to finish the sign-up process.
  • We will also send you an email to welcome you to Gracious Garments.

Can I add multiple email addresses to login to my account?

To make sure that your account is always secure, multiple email addresses cannot be added. However, you can replace your current email address with a new one.


Why do I need to provide my email address to sign up?


Your email address helps us provide timely information to you about your order. It also acts as your username and helps you to login.


How can I deactivate my account?


Why not just let it be and come back to us whenever you want.

Would my account be deactivated if I don’t use it for a long time?


We miss you when you don’t visit us enough but we never deactivate your account.

Gracious Garments Express

What is Gracious Garments Express?

Gracious Garments Express is our premium delivery service which guarantees delivery within 24 hours in Karachi and 48 hours in Lahore and Islamabad. It also guarantees top quality and no out of stock cancellations.


Is Gracious Garments Express available for all products?


Gracious Garments Express is only available for selected products. To find out if a product is available with Gracious Garments Express or not simply look for the Gracious Garments Express logo on the product page. Use can also use the Gracious Garments Express filter to only see items available with Gracious Garments Express.


Why is Gracious Garments Express not available for my location?


Faster delivery options may not be available at your location due to courier related reasons but we’re trying to expand our services to include more places. Stay tuned for updates!

Is faster service options like Gracious Garments Express available for returns?


No. However, we try our best to have your item(s) picked-up by our couriers & get you the replacement as soon as possible.
