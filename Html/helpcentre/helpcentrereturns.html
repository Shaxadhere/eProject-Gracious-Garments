How to returns

1-Contact us to create your return request

2-Fill in the returns form you received with your order

3-Pack your Gracious Garments parcel securely, with the product in the original undamaged manufacturer's packaging as delivered to you

4-Attach the return label that you received with your order to the top of the parcel (ensure that delivery label is covered)

5-Head to your nearest TCS drop-off point or wait for collection by our pick-up service

6-We will send you a tracking number via SMS

7-Contact us to create your return request
